About Wordle Page

Wordle is a fun and challenging puzzle game where players have six attempts to guess a five-letter word. Here's how to play:

  1. Each guess must be a valid five-letter word. Hit the enter button to submit.
  2. After each guess, the color of the letters changes to show how close your guess was to the word.
  3. A green letter means the letter is in the word and in the correct spot.
  4. A yellow letter means the letter is in the word but in the wrong spot.
  5. A gray letter means the letter is not in the word in any spot.
  6. Use the clues from the colored letters to make your next guess.
  7. You have six tries to guess the correct word and win the game.

Enjoy playing Wordle and challenge your friends to see who can guess the word with the fewest attempts!
